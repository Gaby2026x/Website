Contractor Requirements

To maintain Approved Contractor Status, applicants must provide the following minimum compliance items.

Compliance Standards

  • Active General Liability Insurance (COI verified, $1M minimum)
  • State trade licensing (where required)
  • OSHA compliance confirmation
  • W-9 submission
  • Legal U.S. work authorization
  • Verifiable field experience

Approval does not guarantee work allocation.
