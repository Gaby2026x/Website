Contractor Code of Conduct

Approved contractors must meet high standards of professionalism, compliance and quality.

1. Professional Conduct

  • Act respectfully toward clients.
  • Maintain professional communication.
  • Represent Crestara Construction Autority professionally.

2. Compliance

  • Follow OSHA standards, safety regulations, and site rules.
  • Wear appropriate PPE.
  • Maintain in-date compliance documentation.

3. Quality Standards

  • Deliver work to agreed specification.
  • Avoid shortcuts and report issues immediately.
  • Correct defects where workmanship falls below standard.

4. Ethical Standards

  • No bribery or inducements.
  • No direct solicitation of company clients.
  • No false documentation.

5. Reliability

  • Attend site as scheduled and meet deadlines.
  • Provide notice of delays.
  • Maintain proactive communication with site contacts.

Failure to comply may result in suspension or removal from the Approved Network.
